Join the Society

  • Who we are

    We are a new group of Santamarians who have decided to create a community that will influence the future of technology. Everyone knows how technilogically dependant our world has become but how many of us really know how it all works? We aim to bring together a community who is interested in tinkering with anything technologically related.

  • What we believe in

    > Creativity
    > Open Source
    > Freedom of Information
    > Fun
    > Equality

  • What we want to do

    > Game Jams
    > Code Nights
    > Hackathons
    > Student Teaching Sessions
    > Student Group Projects
    > Your ideas!

  • Where you fit in

    Do you want to make friends? Learn how to better use your computer? Learn how to code? Make video games? Create websites? Create Apps?

    Even if you aren't a Computer Science student you are always welcome to become a member and join in on the fun. We are open, honest, and here to better our lives and those around us through technology.
